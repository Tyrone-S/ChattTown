Throughout him and his sons journey he would change in many ways for the better. While also fighting to leave his former self in the past. Along the way he had to face many gods and legends from the Norse pantheon, some were friends and others were foes. Unbeknownst to him all of him and his sons actions were a part of something way bigger than them, Ragnorok.
